Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does it take to complete a website?

Project timelines vary depending on the package and complexity. Our Starter Websites typically take 5-7 days, Business Websites 10-14 days, and Premium Websites 3-4 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

Do I need to provide content for my website?

Yes, you'll need to provide the core content (text and images) for your website. However, we can offer guidance on content strategy and structure. For premium clients, we also offer copywriting and content creation services at an additional cost.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer monthly maintenance plans to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. These plans include regular backups, security monitoring, and technical support. We'll discuss these options after your project is completed.

Can I update the website myself after it's completed?

Yes! We build websites with user-friendly content management systems that allow you to make basic updates yourself. We also provide training and documentation to help you manage your content confidently.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. We typically require a 50% deposit to begin the project, with the remaining balance due upon completion before the website goes live.
